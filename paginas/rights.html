Protection Laws To Women

  • Law Nº 11.340, August 7th, 2006. – Law Maria da Penha

    • 1st Art.: To curb and prevent domestic and family violence against women; Provides for the creation of Domestic and Family Violence Courts against Women; And establishes measures of assistance and protection to women in situations of domestic and family violence.
    2nd Art.: Every woman, regardless of class, race, ethnicity, sexual orientation, income, culture, educational level, age and religion, enjoys the fundamental rights inherent to the human being, being assured the opportunities and facilities to live without violence, to preserve her health Physical and mental well-being and its moral, intellectual and social improvement.
    3rd Art.: The conditions for the effective exercise of the rights to life, safety, health, food, education, culture, housing, access to justice, sport, leisure, work, citizenship, Freedom, dignity, respect and family and community coexistence.

  • Art. 121 Law Decree nº 2.848, December 7th, 1940

    • It includes feminicide in the role of heinous crimes.

  • 1st Art. Law nº 8.072, July 25th, 1990

    • It envisages femicide as a qualifying circumstance for the crime of homicide.

  • Law nº 13.239, December 30th, 2015

    • It deals with the offer and the accomplishment, within the scope of the unique health system - sus, plastic surgery repairing sequels of injuries caused by acts of violence against women.

  • Law nº 12.015, August 7th, 2009

    • Geram prisão e/ou multa:
    Art. 213: Constranger someone, through violence or serious threat, to have a carnal conjunction or to practice or allow him to practice another libidinous act.
    § 1st: If the conduct results in serious bodily injury or if the victim is less than 18 (eighteen) or greater than 14 (fourteen) years.
    § 2nd: If conduct is death.
    Art. 215.: Having a carnal conjunction or practicing another libidinous act with someone through fraud or other means that prevents or hinders the free expression of will of the victim.
    Unique paragraph: If the crime is committed in order to obtain economic advantage, a fine also applies.
